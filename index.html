About Us

TuxCon is a free community event about free and open-source software for Internet of Things, mobile, embedded and wearable devices. The twitter hashtag of the event is #TuxCon.

Conference is scheduled during the first day of the event (11 July) at International Fair Plovdiv. Workshops will take place at Olimex training during the second day of the event (12 July).

Call for Papers

We are looking for outstanding submissions for presentations (30 min) and lightning talks (10 min) related to free and open source software for mobile, embedded and wearable devices. Topics related to GNU/Linux distributions, Android, Firefox OS, SailfishOS and Tizen are preferred.

Please submit your abstract proposals to info@tuxcon.mobi. Call for Papers (CfP) closes on 14 June 2015.
